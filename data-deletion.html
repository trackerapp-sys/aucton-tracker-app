Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2024

How to Delete Your Data

Since our application does not store any of your personal data on external servers, data deletion is simple:

Option 1: Close the Application

All your data is stored locally in your browser session. Simply close the application and all data will be automatically deleted.

Option 2: Clear Browser Data

If you want to ensure complete deletion:

  • Open your browser settings
  • Navigate to "Privacy and Security"
  • Click "Clear browsing data"
  • Select "Cached images and files" and "Site data"
  • Click "Clear data"

Option 3: Revoke Facebook Permissions

To remove our application's access to your Facebook data:

  • Go to your Facebook Settings
  • Click "Apps and Websites"
  • Find "Facebook API Tracker" in your active apps
  • Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions

Contact

If you need assistance with data deletion, contact us at: your-email@domain.com

Note: We never store your personal data on our servers, so no server-side data deletion is required.
