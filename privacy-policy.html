Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

1. Information We Collect

Our Facebook API Tracker application collects the following information when you grant permission:

  • Basic profile information (name, email, user ID)
  • Pages you have liked
  • Your posts and their engagement metrics
  • Friends count

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Displaying your Facebook data within the application
  • Providing analytics about your Facebook activity
  • Allowing you to export your own data

3. Data Storage and Security

We do not store any of your Facebook data on our servers. All data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Only stored in your local browser session
  • Never transmitted to any external servers except Facebook's API
  • Deleted when you close the application

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties. Your data remains exclusively on your local device.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we process
  • Delete all locally stored data by closing the application
  • Revoke Facebook permissions at any time through your Facebook settings

6. Facebook API Compliance

Our application complies with Facebook's Platform Policies and only accesses data you explicitly permit through the Facebook login process.
